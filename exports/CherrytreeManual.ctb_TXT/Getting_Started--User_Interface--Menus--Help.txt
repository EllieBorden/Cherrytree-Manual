Help

    There are several ways of finding help with Cherrytree. The simplest is to just press the F1 key. Most are accessible from the help menu. If you have problems or questions that are not answered in the user manual, you can post a query on the maintainer's website at: 
http://www.giuspen.com/cherrytree/ . 

We will try our very best to answer your question.
